Facebook -   an online social networking service. Users may create a personal profile, add other users as friends, exchange messages, post status updates and photos, and receive notifications when others update their profiles. Additionally, users may join common-interest user groups, organized by workplace, school or college, or other characteristics.

You Have a "Facebook Page" for East End Eatz.
Pages are for businesses, brands and organizations to share their stories and connect with people. Like profiles, you can customize Pages by posting stories, hosting events, adding apps and more. People who like your Page and their friends can get updates in News Feed.

You can create and manage a Page from your personal account.



You can manage interactions on the Page:   Like / Follow other people and pages,  Post Comments, Post status updates, Post Pictures.

People Can Also Interact with Your Page - Like / Follow,  Post Comments, Post Pictures,  Send a Private Message,  Change Profile Photo, Change Cover Photo.

When you interact with your page, the activity is likely to show up in followers and friends newsfeed -  So they will see the activity you just took (posted a comment, posted a picture, checked-in at a location, commented on a followers' comment.).
